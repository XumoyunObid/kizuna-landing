Our story

What does Kizuna mean?

Kizuna (絆) is the Japanese word for bond. We chose this name not as a brand, but as our purpose — to bring people, cultures and opportunities closer together. Whether you are planning your first trip to Japan or expanding your business across borders, Kizuna is your trusted bridge.


Founded in Istanbul with deep roots in Japanese culture and business, we have spent over 12 years operating at the intersection of travel, consulting, culture and international trade — serving individuals, families, businesses and institutions across Turkey, Japan, the Middle East and beyond.

"Strong bonds dissolve distances and reveal real opportunities."

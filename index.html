Our story

What does Kizuna mean?

Kizuna (絆) is the Japanese word for bond. We chose this name not as a brand, but as our purpose — to bring people, cultures and opportunities closer together. Whether you are planning your first trip to Japan or expanding your business across borders, Kizuna is your trusted bridge.


Founded in Istanbul with deep roots in Japanese culture and business, we operate at the intersection of travel, consulting, culture and international trade — serving individuals, families, businesses and institutions across Turkey, Japan, the Middle East and beyond.

"Strong bonds dissolve distances and reveal real opportunities."

Kizuna · Istanbul, Turkey · www.kizuna.com.tr


Mission: To bring people, cultures and opportunities together by building trust-based, lasting relationships — delivering high-standard service and sustainable value in every field we operate.

Vision: To become a pioneering global brand that represents trust, cooperation and cultural exchange — connecting people and institutions across borders.

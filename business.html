Cross-Border Business

Your bridge between Türkiye and Japan

Kizuna facilitates business relationships between Turkish and Japanese companies. From initial market research to ongoing partnership management, we provide end-to-end support for organisations looking to expand across borders.

Trade & Export Facilitation

We connect Turkish exporters with Japanese buyers and vice versa. Our team handles market research, product matching, trade documentation guidance, and introductions to qualified partners in the target market.

Business Delegation Management

End-to-end management of trade delegations — from scheduling meetings with potential partners and government officials to arranging venues, interpreters, and cultural briefings for your team.

Partnership Development

Long-term relationship building between Turkish and Japanese organisations. We identify the right partners, facilitate initial meetings, and provide ongoing support as the relationship develops into a productive partnership.

Corporate Events & MICE

Organisation of business conferences, trade fairs, corporate retreats, and incentive travel in Japan. Complete logistics, venue selection, speaker coordination, and cultural programme design.
